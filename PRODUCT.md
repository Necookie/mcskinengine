# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users
Minecraft players, builders, and creators looking for a fast, intuitive custom skin designer and generator.

## Product Purpose
An interactive web-based utility for editing and generating custom Minecraft skins. It combines a manual 2D pixel-art drawing canvas and 3D preview with edge-rendered AI textures to streamline the skin customization workflow. Success means providing players with a responsive, zero-friction, tactile pipeline to design, preview, and download custom skins.

## Positioning
A high-density, voxel-inspired custom skin designer and AI studio for Minecraft creators.

## Brand Personality
Retro-modern, tactile-voxel, high-contrast, and gaming-heritage (Minecraft inspired). The vibe feels like a clean developer sandbox: structured, responsive, and functional, yet playful.

## Anti-references
- Floating island card architecture (unconnected rounded cards with excessive gaps)
- Generic corporate SaaS templates (cream-colored layout with tiny grey uppercase Kickers)
- Smooth circular gradients and heavy drop shadows acting as visual separators

## Design Principles
- **Grid-Aligned Structure:** Panels sit in a clean, space-efficient docked or bento split-screen alignment with sharp geometry rather than floaty cards.
- **Color-Block Visual Anchors:** Monochrome utility controls ensure tool focus, while flat pastel color blocks clearly demarcate layout parameters.
- **Physical Tactility:** Interactive controls simulate mechanical actions (e.g. active offset pressing down) to reinforce the retro-gaming heritage.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratios of at least 4.5:1 for body and utility labels against their background.
- Respect reduced motion media queries by disabling active rotating animations.
